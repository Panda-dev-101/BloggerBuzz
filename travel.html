The Beautiful Ratti Gali Lake, Neelum Valley, AJK | By Aheed
Blogger Buzz • 1 week ago
Visiting Ratti Gali Lake in Azad Jammu & Kashmir was like stepping into a dream. Nestled at an altitude of around 12,130 feet, the lake mesmerized me with its crystal-clear, glacial waters reflecting the surrounding snow-capped peaks. The journey to the lake was an adventure in itself—

Paradise of Kashmir | Neelum Valley
Blogger Buzz • 3 days ago
Neelum Valley, often called the "Paradise of Kashmir," is a breathtakingly beautiful region in Azad Jammu & Kashmir. Stretching along the Neelum River, this valley is known for its lush green meadows, dense forests, and snow-capped peaks. The charming villages, such as Keran, Sharda, and Arang Kel, offer mesmerizing views and a peaceful retreat for nature lovers.

Naran Kaghan | Pakistan
Blogger Buzz • 2 weeks ago
Naran is a picturesque town in the Kaghan Valley of Pakistan, renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, towering mountains, and lush greenery. Located at an altitude of around 8,200 feet, it serves as a gateway to many stunning destinations, including Saif-ul-Muluk Lake, Lulusar Lake, and Babusar Top.

Artificial lake| Banjosa Lake
Blogger Buzz • 1 month ago
Banjosa Lake is a stunning artificial lake located near Rawalakot in Azad Jammu & Kashmir, offering breathtaking views and a peaceful retreat for nature lovers. Surrounded by dense pine forests and lush green hills, the lake’s crystal-clear waters reflect the beauty of the landscape, creating a picture-perfect setting.
